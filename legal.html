Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

Welcome to PhysioNet. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use our iOS application.

By using the app, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Who This Policy Covers

This policy applies to:

  • Patients using the app to discover physiotherapists, book appointments, access exercise programs, and read articles.
  • Physiotherapists using the app to register, verify credentials, manage appointments, create programs, and manage profile/availability.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of data depending on your role and usage:

Account and identity data:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Encrypted password credentials (processed by our authentication provider)

Profile and professional data (physiotherapist accounts):

  • Gender
  • Date of birth
  • Place of work
  • Years of experience
  • Consultation fee
  • About/bio
  • Profile image

Verification documents (physiotherapist accounts):

  • ID proof image/document
  • License/registration proof image/document

Patient profile and wellness data:

  • Gender
  • Date of birth
  • Address/location fields entered by user
  • Exercise progress data (completion, watched seconds, optional notes/pain level)

Appointment and service data:

  • Appointment date/time
  • Appointment status
  • Address text
  • Contact phone
  • Session instructions/notes

Location data:

  • Approximate or precise location only when permission is granted, used for nearby physiotherapist discovery and relevant booking options.

Content interaction data:

  • Article views/bookmarks/ratings/read time
  • Feed personalization signals

Device and technical data:

  • App logs/errors
  • Session metadata and security events required for fraud prevention and service reliability

3. How We Collect Data

We collect data:

  • Directly from forms you complete in the app.
  • From actions you perform (booking, profile edits, program usage, article interactions).
  • From files you upload (profile image, ID/licensing proofs).
  • From iOS permission APIs when you allow location/photo access.

4. Why We Use Data

We use data to:

  • Create and manage user accounts.
  • Authenticate users securely.
  • Verify physiotherapist eligibility and credentials.
  • Enable appointment booking, scheduling, and status updates.
  • Display relevant physiotherapists by location and specialization.
  • Provide exercise programs, progress tracking, and session workflows.
  • Personalize article recommendations and measure engagement.
  • Provide customer support and respond to issues.
  • Detect abuse, fraud, unauthorized access, and policy violations.
  • Meet legal, regulatory, and safety obligations.

5. Legal Basis / Consent

Where required, we rely on:

  • Your consent (for optional permissions and certain processing).
  • Performance of contract (to provide app services).
  • Legitimate interests (security, service improvement, abuse prevention).
  • Legal obligations (recordkeeping/compliance).

6. Sharing of Data

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with:

  • Cloud hosting and database providers.
  • Secure file storage providers.
  • Analytics/monitoring providers needed for app reliability.
  • Service providers acting on our instructions under confidentiality and security obligations.
  • Law enforcement/regulators where legally required.

7. Data Storage and Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards, including:

  • Access controls and authentication checks.
  • Encrypted network transport (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Role-based access rules and backend authorization policies.
  • Limited privileged server-side operations for account deletion and compliance workflows.

No system is 100% secure. We continuously work to protect your data.

8. Data Retention

We keep data only as long as needed for:

  • Service operation
  • Legal obligations
  • Legitimate business/security needs

Typical retention examples:

  • Active account data: retained while account is active.
  • Verification documents: retained while account remains active and verification/compliance requires it.
  • Logs/security telemetry: retained for limited operational/security periods.
  • Deletion requests: processed as described below.

9. Your Rights and Controls

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete account/data
  • Restrict or object to certain processing
  • Data portability (where applicable)

In-app controls include:

  • Edit profile
  • Log out
  • Delete account (for logged-in users)

10. Account and Data Deletion

You can request deletion directly from the app profile screen when logged in.

Deletion removes account-linked data subject to:

  • Certain operational constraints (for example, active booked appointments may require cancellation/completion first).
  • Legal retention requirements where applicable.

If you cannot delete in-app, contact support at support.physionet@gmail.com.

11. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13 (or higher minimum age where required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children below the applicable age threshold.

12. International Data Transfers

Data may be processed in countries outside your region. We apply safeguards required by applicable law.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will revise the “Last Updated” date and, when required, provide additional notice.
