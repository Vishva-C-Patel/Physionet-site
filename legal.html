Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

Welcome to PhysioNet (“we”, “us”, “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use our iOS application.

By using the app, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Who This Policy Covers

  • Patients using the app to discover physiotherapists, book appointments, access exercise programs, and read articles.
  • Physiotherapists using the app to register, verify credentials, manage appointments, create programs, and manage profiles.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account Data: Name, email address, phone number, encrypted credentials
  • Professional Data: Gender, DOB, workplace, experience, fees, bio, profile image
  • Verification Documents: ID proof, license/registration documents
  • Patient Data: Gender, DOB, address, exercise progress, notes
  • Appointment Data: Date, time, status, contact details, session notes
  • Location Data: Used only with permission for nearby discovery
  • Content Data: Article interactions, engagement signals
  • Technical Data: Logs, session metadata, security events

3. How We Collect Data

  • Forms filled in the app
  • User actions (booking, edits, usage)
  • Uploaded files
  • iOS permissions (location/photos)

4. Why We Use Data

  • Account creation and authentication
  • Appointment booking and scheduling
  • Physiotherapist verification
  • Personalization and recommendations
  • Customer support
  • Fraud prevention and security
  • Legal compliance

5. Legal Basis

  • User consent
  • Contract performance
  • Legitimate interests
  • Legal obligations

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Data may be shared with:

  • Cloud/database providers
  • Storage providers
  • Analytics/monitoring tools
  • Authorities when legally required

7. Data Security

We use encryption (HTTPS), access controls, and secure backend systems. No system is 100% secure.

8. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: retained while active
  • Verification data: retained for compliance
  • Logs: retained for limited periods

9. Your Rights
